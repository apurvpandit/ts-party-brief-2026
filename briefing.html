What was actually done: the guest list (~280 names) was extracted in full from the PDF you supplied. Every name was categorised by relevance to the stated brief using the title/organisation already printed on the list. What wasn't done: individual web verification on all ~280 names. Where facts might be stale: several titles on the guest list itself appear to be out of date (Graham Ruddick, and possibly Anup Ratnaparkhi) — people move jobs between when a list like this is compiled and the event itself. Treat every listed title as a starting point to confirm in conversation, not a fixed fact.
